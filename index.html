The Author

Welcome, traveler. I am Robby Choudhary, a storyteller dedicated to creating worlds that feel alive beyond the page. My stories explore forgotten civilizations, mythic powers, ancient mysteries, scientific imagination, and the choices that shape heroes and monsters alike. Every world I create follows its own logic, history, and laws. Whether through fantasy kingdoms, cosmic civilizations, or lost legends, my goal is to invite readers into places that feel real enough to remember long after the final chapter.

Current Work

Eldrya

First Saga: Genesis

In a world woven from ancient powers and forgotten truths, destinies begin to awaken. The First Saga follows the earliest steps of a greater journey, where mysteries buried beneath history slowly reveal themselves, and the fate of Eldrya begins to turn. Ancient forces stir. Old legends return. And a new generation stands at the edge of a changing world.

"Every world begins as a whisper before it becomes a legend."
